PRIMARY SCHOOL

From 2009 until 2014, I attended this school to further my education. There is a vehicle, a motorcycle and a school van that are utilised to get there; it's fifteen kilometres from my house.

SECONDARY SCHOOL

SEKOLAH MENENGAH KEBANGSAAN DESA CEMPAKA


From 2015 till 2019, I attended this secondary school. The Arabic language is emphasised in this school's curriculum. Since there isn't a dorm at the institution, pupils must travel each day from home to school. My most treasured memories are from this area, and I will always have a particular place in my heart for my high school pals.

UNIVERSITY

After finishing high school, I continued my studies at Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM) Cawangan Negeri Sembilan Kampus Rembau. I studied the course Information Management for five semesters. This year will be my last semester here before becoming alumni.
